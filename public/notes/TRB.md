Constituted in 1987

Objectives

- To recruit qualified and competent teachers for government school and colleges.

Vision

1. To Upgrade and improve the system and procedure to attract the best talented peoples.

2. Computerization and networking of all recruitment activities.

Functions

1. Recruit teachers of various categories through appropriate examination. Defines syllabus, question patterns, evaluation and selections.

2. To conduct teachers eligibility test (TET). Development of online application and registration services.
