***Commutative***
- Numbers can be multiplied in any order and yields the same product.

***Associative***
- Numbers can be multiplied in groups of any order, as with parentheses, and yields the same product.

***Identity***
- If you multiply one to any number, n, it yields a product of n.

***Zero product***
- If you multiply any number by zero, it yields a product of zero.

***Distributive***
- The product of a sum or difference equals the sum or difference of the product.
