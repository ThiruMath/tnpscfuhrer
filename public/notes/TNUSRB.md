Tamilnadu Uniform Services Recruitment Board

1. It was Setup in 1991.

2. It consists of a board – Chairman (DGP), Member (ADGP) and 

3. Member Secretary (IG).

Functions

- It conducts recruitment for Sub – inspector of Police (Technical Services), Sub – Inspector, Grade 2 Police Constable, Grade 2 Jail Wardens and Fireman.
