Recognized by the 73rd and 74th Constitutional Amendments (1992), this tier brings democracy to the grassroots level.

### Panchayati Raj (Rural Areas): 

A **three-tier** system for rural governance.  

**Gram Panchayat:** At the village level.  
**Panchayat Samiti**: At the block (taluka) level.  
**Zila Parishad**: At the district level.

### Municipalities (Urban Areas): 

A **three-type** system for urban governance.  

**Nagar Panchayat:** For areas transitioning from rural to urban.  

**Municipal Council (Nagar Palika)**: For smaller cities.  

**Municipal Corporation (Nagar Nigam):** For large metropolitan cities.
