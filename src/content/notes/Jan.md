  

Jan 1 : **DRDO Foundation Day**

- DRDO celebrated the 66th foundation Day on 1st January, 2024.
- It is the Research & Development wing of the **Ministry of Defence**.
- **It was formed in 1958.**

Jan 3 : **77th foundation day of BIS**

- Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) is the National Standards Body of India
- It is a statutory body functioning under the **Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution**.

Jan 4 : **World Braille Day**

- The day commemorates the birth anniversary of **Louis Braille**, the inventor of Braille.

Jan 9 : **Pravasi Bharatiya Divas**

- also known as Non-Resident Indian (NRI) Day
- The day also commemorates the return of Mahatma Gandhi, from South Africa to India in 1915.
- The day was first celebrated in 2003

Jan 12 : **National Youth Day**

- Theme 2024 : "MYBharat - Viksit Bharat@2047- By the Youth, For the Youth”
- commemorate the birth anniversary of Swami Vivekananda who was born on January 12, 1863, in Kolkata.
- Vivekananda delivered his famous speech at the Parliament of the World’s Religions in Chicago in 1893.

Jan 10 to 18 : **Startup India Innovation Week**

- The Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) under Ministry of Commerce and Industry launched the Startup India Innovation Week 2024
- The National Startup Day - **Jan16**, **2024**.

Jan 15 : **76th Army Day**

- It was on this day in 1949 that **Field Marshal Kodandera M Cariappa** became the first Indian Commander-in-Chief of Independent India.

Jan 23 : 127th birth anniversary of Nathaji

- The day is observed as **Parakram Diwas**.

Jan 24:

- **National Girl Child Day**
- The day was first observed by the **Ministry of Women and Child Development in 2008.**

  

- **International Day of Education**
- Theme for 2024 is ‘Learning for Lasting Peace’.
- The day was first observed on January 24, 2019 based on the resolution passed by the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA)

  

- **Uttar Pradesh Foundation Day**
- The United Province was renamed as **Uttar Pradesh** on **January** **24,** **1950**.
- The Uttar Pradesh government declared January 24 as Uttar Pradesh Foundation Day in **May 2017.**

Jan 25 :

- **13th National Voters’ Day**
- The day is observed by the Election Commission of India since 2011.
- 25th January 1950 is the foundation day of the Election Commission of India (ECI)
- NVD 2024 theme - ‘**Nothing Like Voting, I Vote For sure’.**

  

- **National Tourism Day**
- First celebrated in 1948.
- Theme 2024 - "Sustainable Journeys, Timeless Memories."

  

- **54th Himachal Pradesh statehood day**
- Himachal Pradesh came into existence on 25th January 1971 as the 18th state of India.
- Indira Gandhi was the then Prime Minister of India.

Jan 26 :

- **International Customs Day**
- Theme 2024 : **Customs Engaging Traditional and New Partners with Purpose.**

  

- **World Leprosy Day**
- is observed every year on the **last Sunday of Jan**.
- The day aims to create awareness against the stigma attached to the disease.
- Leprosy is also known as Hansen Disease.
- Theme 2024 : “Beat Leprosy”.
- Leprosy is caused by the bacteria **Mycobacterium leprae.**

Jan 30:

- **World Neglected Tropical Disease Day (WNTDD)**
- To create better awareness of the devastating impact of NTDs on the poorest populations around the world.
- Theme 2024 : **Unite.Act**.**Eliminate**.

  

- **Martyrs' Day**
- Shaheed Diwas commemorating the death anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi.
- Mahatma Gandhi was assassinated by Nathuram Vinayak Godse in 1948.
