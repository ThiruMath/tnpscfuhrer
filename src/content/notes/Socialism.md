Socialism refers to a system of total planning, public ownership and state control on economie activities. In socialism, there no freedom for private and there is public ownership of means of production. Socialist economy is also known as 'Planned Economy' or 'Command Economy'. 

[[Karl Marx]] is known as the father of socialism. [[Russia]], [[China]], [[Vietnam]] and [[Cuba]] are examples of socialist economies. But, now there are no absolutely socialist economies.
