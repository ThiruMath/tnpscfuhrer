***Data***  : The numerical representation of facts is called data. 

***Observation*** : Each entry in the data is called an observation. 

***Variable*** :  The quantities which are being considered in a survey are called variables. 

Variables are generally denoted by xi , i =1,2,3,…,n. 

***Frequencies*** :  The number of times, a variable occurs in a given data is called the frequency of that variable. Frequencies are generally denoted as   fi , i = 1,2,3,…,n.
