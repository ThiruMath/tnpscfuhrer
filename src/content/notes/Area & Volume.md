**Area** : is defined as the **number of unit squares** that cover the shape without any gaps or overlaps.

==**Length**== or ==**Width**==

==**Area**== = ==**Length**== **×** ==**Width**==

==**Volume**== = ==**Area**== **×** ==**Height**== or Length × Width × Height

> [!important]  
> Area of Rectangle = Length × Width  

- A square is special case of Rectangle where all side's are equal.

i.e. Length= Width= a

> [!important]  
> Area of Square = a²

**Diagonal**

- **Using Pythagoras Theorem**

**Diagonal of Rectangle**

> [!important]  
> Length ² + Width ² = Diagonal ²  

**Diagonal of Square**

In square length = Width

So Length ² + Length ² = Diagonal ²

> [!important]  
> 2 Length ² = Diagonal ²  

**Circumference of Rectangle and Square**

> [!important]  
> Rectangle = 2 Length + 2 Width  
  
> [!important]  
> Square = 4 Sides
