# Chola Dynasty

The Chola dynasty ruled parts of South India from the 9th to 13th century.

## Important Kings

- Rajaraja Chola
- Rajendra Chola

## Important Places

- Thanjavur
- Gangaikonda Cholapuram

## Architecture

Cholas built magnificent temples such as Brihadeeswara Temple.
